NCR RealPOSTM 72XRT

The RealPOSTM 72XRT is one of our most popular point-of-sale (POS) terminals amongst our retail customers. Its integrated design, high performance, and configuration options let you customize the 72XRT to meet your unique requirements. It’s available with either a 15” or a 17” touchscreen and a choice of Intel®CoreTM processors to give you the performance you can count on now – and in the future.

Its unique EZ-Glide Blade system is a key feature that gives you secure, tool-free access for quick servicing and upgrades so you can focus on what counts – servicing your customers. The rugged enclosure, Intel® processors, long life LCD display and solid state drive (SSD) ensure years of dependable operation. Check out the 72XRT and see why our customers love it.
